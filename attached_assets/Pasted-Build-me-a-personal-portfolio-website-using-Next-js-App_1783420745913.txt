Theme:

Primary color: green (choose a sophisticated shade — e.g. deep emerald or forest green — not a bright/neon green), paired with neutral off-white/charcoal tones for a premium contrast
Consistent accent usage: buttons, links, section highlights, hover states

Pages needed (separate routes):

Home (/)

Strong headline + tagline about who I am
Short intro/summary with CTA buttons to Projects and Blog
Nav bar (Home, About, Projects, Blog, Contact) + footer with social links

tellus.site
